Data Source : Unemployment Insurance Claims 7

Unemployment Insurance Claims is downloaded from US Department of Labor Employment and Training administration website.

To download data from the website, you need to fill in the form online with details  like what type of data you are looking for: National or State.  Beginning Year, Ending Year and what format the output should be.

Web scrape the below website, get the details of the form, including action, method and list of form controls (inputs, etc)

https://oui.doleta.gov/unemploy/claims.asp

Pass the parameters and download the data.
