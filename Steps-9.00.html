COVID Job Loss vs Great Recession:

We compared the new jobs that was created since recession with the Unemployment Initial claims, which will give a closer number towards unemployed. The number of new jobs that was created since recession is about 22.4M. The number of Initial claims since the start of pandemic is 52.6M. This correlation shows that there is a gap of 30M jobs.

We want to conclude our analysis stating that Coronavirus has wiped out all the job gains since the Great recession. We need at least ~30M new jobs to overcome the Pandemic loss.
